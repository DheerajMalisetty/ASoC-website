ASoC

< Begin your Open Source Journey />

Amrita Season of Code is a 5-week long online programme for the students of Amrita, who are new to open source software development, where the registered students contribute to the projects in the ASoC project list. The programme not only helps students to get involved in open source but also prepares them for GSoC too.
